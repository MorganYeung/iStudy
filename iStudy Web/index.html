It is a known fact that studying with another person is one of the most effective ways in studying. Study Buddy allows users to study for assessments through interactive activities on the Amazon Alexa or the Slack App.

iStudy's Features

Auditory Interactive Studying

30% of the population are auditory learners. Study Buddy is a learning tool for more auditory learners.

Visualory Interactive Studying

Study Buddy is a learning tool for visual learners who absorb content better through interactive experiences.
